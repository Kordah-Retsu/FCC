Dr. Kwame Nkrumah

The man who led Ghana to Independence

Dr.Kwame Nkrumah was the first President of the Republic of Ghana

Here's a timeline of Dr Kwame Nkrumah's life:

  • 1909 - Born in Nkroful, Gold Coast (now Ghana).
  • 1945 - Played a significant role in organizing the Fifth Pan-African Congress in Manchester, UK.
  • 1947 - Returned to the Gold Coast and became the General Secretary of the United Gold Coast Convention (UGCC).
  • 1949 - Founded the Convention People's Party (CPP) and began campaigning for immediate independence.
  • 1957 - Led the Gold Coast to independence, becoming the first Prime Minister of Ghana.
  • 1960 - Became the first President of Ghana after it became a republic.
  • 1966 - Overthrown in a military coup while on a state visit to Vietnam and China.
  • 1972 - Died in Bucharest, Romania.
